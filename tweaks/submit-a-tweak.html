Submit-a-Tweak

Share your lovingly-crafted tweaks and mods with the world! —

Hey there my cuties! did you take the time and effort to make your very own tweak/mod? First of all, allow me to thank you and congratulate you! that stuff sure does need some patience! If your heart is huge and kind, you can always share your lovingly creation with the world! Below I have written some instructions in how to submit your very own tweak.

Submit-a-Tweak Formats


Google Drive Project

First of, please do allow me to introduce my newest sharing project! a shared Google Drive folder which you may visit clicking here. Basically all you've gotta do is pack your tweak files (and preferrently add a .txt file inside explaining what your tweak does) and throw them in that folder. Can't get any easier! If you still need help uploading your tweak, please visit This Guide.


Send it to me at my Discord

Don't wanna deal with Google Drive? You can always just DM it to me at Discord @luisarlz. As per instructions written above, please package your tweak first and if possible, add your .txt file so we all know what your tweak does.


Upload it to the TOP Resource Discord

Don't wanna deal with my shitface nor with Google Drive? Join the TOP Resource Discord and then post your tweak at the #submit-a-tweak channel under the Tweaks section. As per instructions written above, please package your tweak first and if possible, add your .txt file so we all know what your tweak does.


Disclaimer

Please do note that all tweaks submitted to me in any of the ways above will most likely be put for free use in this website, with the proper credits given to the creator (unless you specifically want to remain anonymous please let me know).
