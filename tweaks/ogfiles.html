About HD Terrain Tweak

Download Day Ver. Download Night Ver. #1 Download Night Ver. #2 Preview

  Folders to backup before installing: texture/terrain and texture/water 

HD Terrain Mod is a floor textures tweak that "updates" all your floor textures to an "HD" version (according to myself). I mean, it's not really HD but I think it looks kind of nice.

Just like in the original version of these files, I created the 'Day' version and the 'Night' version since this game happens to use colors brighter than all of our futures, hoping it helps your sight rest a bit.
HD Terrain mod is a tweak I created because I wanted to look at a more beautiful game, even if a little, while doing my common game tasks. As usual, I had the idea while doing my common tasks in the toilet.

I based myself off the original 'Night Argent Tweak' as it's a very popular tweak... but I think it happens to not be... very visually nice. Still, this is just an alternative and not meant to replace the original Night Argent Tweak, it's simply something I consider a little improvement.

Back then, I created the Night Argent Tweak due to my eye problems, but at this time I had absolutely no idea how to change colors, so I just made every texture blacker... that's it. However, in this new HD mod, I actually made a careful edit keeping colors properly and whatnot, to turn it into an actual "Night" view (not just blacker colors). I used about 10 minutes of my precious time manually editing the colors, it's been hard. I named this version 'Night Version #1'. But well... since people seem to enjoy simplicity, I also made another version, which, like it's classic version, just makes every texture blacker, and called it 'Night Version #2'. 1 minute of hard work was spent creating this one.

Anyways, I am genuinely hoping you enjoy one of the 3 versions. All terrains are beautiful (except the originals).
Go to your client's folder, texture > terrain. You will find a lot of images there which can be edited with any designing tool. I highly recommend using something like photoshop or similar, as integrated tools like paint might not give you the best results.
Remember it is important to keep the image size intact, as modifying the image size might break the game. Also, as you may notice by now, the floor works in 'Tiles' (little squares that repeat), so whenever you edit it, make sure to make every tile seamless (this meaning every tile has to merge with each other in harmony) otherwise you will get a squary, patchy mess. You can always find tutorials on how to do this by simply looking up 'how to make seamless tiles in photoshop' and similar searches. You can always find seamless images in google as well which will give you a good helping hand!
In the case you see only completely white textures in your floor, it means that the tweak was not correctly installed. Please make sure you're not taking folders or images out of the folders and also make sure you're not changing the image's format.
