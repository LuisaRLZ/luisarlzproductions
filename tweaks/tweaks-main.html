All Tweaks for Tales of Pirates Servers

The true modern Tweaks Sharing 2022
A Tweak is what would be known in other games as Mods - any sort of modification to your game client that improves your gameplay experience without harmfully affecting others or the server's wellbeing.

A long time ago, tweaks were considered illegal and/or disrespectful to use, however due to the community's effort to make them as available as possible, they sort of became semi-official as long they do not purposefully ruin the game for others.

Usually, files are directly modified to attain certain desired results, and then are shared around for you to paste in your own game client and enjoy the same modification. Here in this website, I gather a good amount of Tweaks made for different servers for you to enjoy.

You may take any tweak you wish, use it, modify it and make your own versions too! However we are thankful if you credit the original author of the tweak.

If you encounter any problem with any of the Tweaks, please contact me at Discord @LuisaRLZ#3429
