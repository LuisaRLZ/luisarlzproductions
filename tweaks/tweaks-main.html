A Tweak is what would be known in other games as Mods - any sort of modification to your game client that improves your gameplay experience without harmfully affecting others or the server's wellbeing.

A long time ago, tweaks were considered illegal and/or disrespectful to use, however due to the community's effort to make them as available as possible, they sort of became semi-official as long they do not purposefully ruin the game for others.

Usually, files are directly modified to attain certain desired results, and then are shared around for you to paste in your own game client and enjoy the same modification. Here in this website, I gather a good amount of Tweaks made for different servers for you to enjoy.

You may take any tweak you wish, use it, modify it and make your own versions too! However we are thankful if you credit the original author of the tweak.

If you encounter any problem with any of the Tweaks, please contact me at Discord @LuisaRLZ#3429

Watch the video above starting from minute 1:23 for fast instructions in most Tweaks Installing!

  Before installing any tweak, make sure to create a backup of your files, just in case anything goes wrong!  

  If something does go wrong, just put the backed files back or run Version Check from launcher  

Almost every tweak / mod in this page is installed the same way: Download File by clicking any Download Button, then Uncompress the files and put them directly into your Server's client folder.

Tip 1: If you don't know where your Server's Client Folder is, just right-click the icon that launches the game, then select "Go to file location".

Tip 2: If you can't uncompress the files, you may be missing an uncompressor software. Download one of the following (they're free) and you'll be able to open the files.

Download WinRar | Download WinZip | Download 7-zip

Tip 3: The tweak is nice, but you feel you could make a better version? find more interesting information at Tweaking Resources and make your very own Mod! Also if you want to share it with the world, make sure to check my Submitting Form!

With all of this said, however, some main servers have their very own way to install Tweaks, usually managed from launcher. Make sure to Visit the Tweaks Page for the server you play!
